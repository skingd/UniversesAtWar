# This is the overview of usage and project intent

`Button Label` How to Play

Welcome! This is an unofficial rule set that translates the universe of BattleTech to be compatible with Legions Imperialis.

This allows you to play with any models suitable for the BattleTech game with (or without) models from Legion Imperialis.

What you'll need: 

- A copy of the Legions Imperials rules.
- Any additional rules needed for a Legions Imperialis army.
- Models to represent the detachments found here and/or in the Legions Imperialis source books.
- One or more friends who love to play miniature war games.
- A space to play (the dining room table works great!).

You can find any of these at your local game store and many of them at your favorite online retailer. Both Catalyst Games Lab and Games Workshop produce the models you'll need.
